Rainer Alvarado

University of Stanford

Outfielder from the New York City region. Played 4 years for the High School for Environmental Studies. During his senior year in high school Alvarado batted .367 with 12 Homeruns and 46 RBI, leading the senior circuit. He received many offers from schools including top baseball programs such as Vanderbilt and Florida. Alvarado elected to attend the University of Stanford, where he posted a league best .340 average as a freshmen while hitting 23 Homeruns. He ended up getting drafted by the Blue Jays in the 4th round of the 2015 MLB Amateur Draft, but did not sign with the team. He is now currently preparing for his second season with the Stanford Cardinals.
